Proteins help build muscle mass and increase the activity of each cell. When the kidneys are performing poorly, they cannot process all the protein that enters the body with food. Therefore, you will need to reduce the amount of protein at the expense of carbohydrates and fatsfrom which you can get the necessary energy. The dietitian will need to find the right balance of protein, carbohydrates and fat that is right for you.
